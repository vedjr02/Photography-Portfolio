About

Behind the lens

I'm Vedant — a photographer based in Mumbai, India. What started as curiosity behind a viewfinder became a way of seeing. I chase golden hours, quiet streets, and the small details most people walk past.

Every frame is an attempt to hold something fleeting — a mood, a memory, a sliver of light that won't come back the same way twice.
